आरोग्य पर्यटन

What "Wellness Tourism" actually means here.

Not spa weekends. A national repositioning — every existing experience reframed through the lens of restoration, breath, and altitude.

Himalayan Yoga & Meditation

Sunrise sessions facing the Annapurna and Langtang ranges; monastery-led retreats in Pokhara, Kathmandu, and the high valleys.

Hot Springs & Ayurveda

Tatopani and Bahundanda's natural hot springs, paired with Ayurvedic resort treatments rooted in Himalayan herbal traditions.

Altitude & Forest Therapy

Clean high-altitude air across Khumbu and Mustang, and rhododendron-forest walks prescribed as much as photographed.

Spiritual & Monastery Stays

Pashupatinath, Lumbini, Boudhanath, and Khumbu's high monasteries — pilgrimage reframed as a slower, restorative kind of travel.
